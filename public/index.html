About 007 First Light

007 First Light is a third-person action-espionage game developed by IO Interactive, the studio behind the Hitman series. Released on May 27, 2026, it serves as a James Bond origin story — following a 26-year-old Bond fresh out of MI6 academy on the mission that earns him his 00 status.

The game features "wide-linear" sandbox missions across 9 global locations: Iceland, Malta, Slovakia, London, Monaco, Tokyo, Dubai, Vietnam, and Antarctica. Each mission offers multiple approach paths — stealth, social engineering, or direct combat — much like IO's Hitman games but with a more cinematic narrative structure.

Playtime: 14–20 hours for main story | 35–40+ hours for 100% completion
Difficulty: No difficulty-related trophies — play on any setting
Missables: None — Chapter Select available after completing the game

Key Features

Systemic Stealth

Social stealth with disguises, eavesdropping, pickpocketing, and bluffing. Gadget-based infiltration with the Q-Watch.

Cinematic Combat

Hand-to-hand brawling mixed with gunplay. The "License to Kill" mechanic unlocks lethal force only when enemies draw weapons first.

Q-Branch Gadgets

Laser Pen, Dart Phone, Shockwave Camera, Missile Pen, Smoke Pods — powered by collectible resources, not cooldowns.

Vehicle Sequences

Drive the iconic Aston Martin through cinematic car chases across mountains, city streets, and airfield runways.

TacSim Mode

Post-game tactical simulator with bite-sized challenge missions, scoring, leaderboards, and gadget upgrades.

92 Collectibles

36 Cards, 23 Intel Files, 14 Mementos, 10 Postcards, and 9 Legacy items hidden across all missions.
