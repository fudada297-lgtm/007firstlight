007 First Light — All 92 Collectible Locations

Complete guide to finding every collectible in the game. There are 5 types totaling 92 items:

Cards 36 Playing Cards

Trophy: Casino Royale

Intel 23 MI6 Files

Trophy: For Your Eyes Only

Mementos 14 Mementos

Trophy: Recovered Assets

Postcards 10 Postcards

Trophy: The World Is Not Enough

Legacy 9 Legacy Items

Trophy: A Legacy of Spycraft

Important: Nothing is missable. Use Chapter Select after completing the story to clean up any missed items. Collectibles save instantly upon pickup — you can grab one and immediately exit to the main menu.
