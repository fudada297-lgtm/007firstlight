007 First Light — Weapons & Gadgets Guide

Bond's arsenal includes Q-Branch gadgets, standard firearms, and environmental weapons. Here's the complete breakdown.

Q-Branch Gadgets

Gadgets are Bond's most versatile tools. Most run on two resource meters: Electronic (recharge from batteries, car cells, power outlets) and Chemical (refill from cleaning supplies, hand sanitizer, lab equipment).

Q-Watch / Q-Lens

The central multi-tool and your most-used gadget. Functions include:

  • Q-Lens Scan: Highlight enemies, collectibles, keys, clues, and NPC outlines through walls. Activate frequently — it's your most powerful information tool.
  • Hacking: Hack enemy grenades before they explode (unlocks Damp Squib trophy), disable security systems, open electronic locks.
  • Laser Cutter: Cut through padlocks, vents, and certain barriers (requires Laser Strap equipped).
  • Poison Darts: Silent ranged takedown option.
  • Flash/Smoke: Area denial and escape tools.
Pro Tip: The Q-Watch must be manually equipped with specific modules at Q-Lab between missions. Don't forget to load out before heading into the field.

Dart Phone

A smartphone modified to fire poisonous or knockout darts. Key uses:

  • Silent takedowns at medium range
  • Makes targets nauseous — excellent for pickpocketing setups (target becomes distracted)
  • Non-lethal option for ghost/pacifist challenge runs

Laser Strap (Laser Pen)

A wrist-mounted laser with multiple functions:

  • Cutting: Destroy padlocks, open vents, cut through breakable barriers
  • Blinding: Daze enemies temporarily (creates takedown openings)
  • Disarming: Shoot weapons from enemy hands at close range (unlocks Disarming Personality trophy)
Critical: You MUST manually equip the Laser Strap at Q-Lab in Chapter 9 (Out of the Ashes). Many collectibles in Chapters 10-12 require it. If you forget, you'll need to replay those sections.

Smoke Pods

Throwable devices that create a dense smoke screen. Essential for:

  • Breaking line of sight during escapes
  • Crossing open areas undetected in stealth runs
  • Covering your retreat when health is low
  • Ghost/pacifist challenge completions

Missile Pen

An explosive pen that functions as a guided projectile. Best for loud combat encounters. Deals significant area damage. Limited ammunition — save for armored enemies and boss fights.

Shockwave Camera

A modified camera that emits a concussive blast. Knocks back nearby enemies, destroys weak structures, and can trigger environmental hazards. Excellent crowd control tool when surrounded.

Flash Mine Earphones

Earphones modified into deployable flash mines. Plant them as traps, then lure enemies into them. Blinds and stuns anyone in the blast radius. Great for setting up ambushes or covering escape routes.

Instinct / Focus System

Not a gadget per se, but a core ability. Instinct is a scarce resource used for:

  • Bluffing: Talk your way past suspicious guards
  • Luring: Draw enemies to specific positions
  • Focus: Slow-motion aiming for precision shots during combat

Use Instinct sparingly — it doesn't regenerate quickly. Save it for critical moments.

Firearms

Pistols

Bond's standard sidearm. Reliable, accurate, and available in silenced variants for stealth missions.

  • Silenced Pistol: Your go-to for stealth runs. Headshots are one-hit kills on unarmored enemies.
  • Standard Pistol: Higher damage, louder. Use when stealth is already broken.

Assault Rifles

Picked up from enemies in combat-heavy sections. High rate of fire, large magazines. Best used in loud encounters where sustained firepower is needed. Inaccurate at long range — burst fire for best results.

Shotguns

Devastating at close range, useless at distance. Slow rate of fire but each shot can take down multiple enemies. Found on security personnel in later missions.

Weapon Skins

Cosmetic variants available through pre-order bonuses and special editions:

  • Gilded Wraith — IOI account link bonus
  • Deluxe Edition skins — multiple exclusive variants
  • Legacy Edition — includes Golden Gun figurine ($299.99 edition)

Recommended Loadouts

Stealth / Ghost

Gadgets: Dart Phone, Smoke Pod, Laser Strap, Q-Watch
Weapon: Silenced Pistol
Playstyle: Never detected. Use distractions, non-lethal takedowns, and social stealth.

Combat / Loud

Gadgets: Missile Pen, Shockwave Camera, Flash Mine Earphones
Weapon: Assault Rifle (picked up in-mission)
Playstyle: Go loud. Use explosives, area denial, and aggressive flanking.

Hybrid / First Playthrough

Gadgets: Dart Phone, Smoke Pod, Missile Pen
Weapon: Silenced Pistol
Playstyle: Try stealth first, have combat tools ready when things go wrong.
