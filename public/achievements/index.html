007 First Light — Achievement & Trophy Guide

007 First Light has 38 trophies (1 Platinum, 4 Gold, 9 Silver, 24 Bronze). There are no difficulty-related trophies — the entire list can be completed on any difficulty. Platinum is achievable in a single playthrough with Chapter Select cleanup.

Estimated time to Platinum: 35–40 hours
Missable trophies: None — Chapter Select available after completing the story
Difficulty required: Any (no difficulty trophies)
Minimum playthroughs: 1 + Chapter Select cleanup

Platinum Roadmap

  1. First Playthrough (20 hours): Play the campaign on any difficulty. Enjoy the story. Don't worry about collectibles or challenges — just learn the systems.
  2. Chapter Select Cleanup (10–15 hours): Use our Collectibles Guide to sweep up all 92 items. Replay missions for combat trophies. Use our Safe Codes guide to speed through locked doors.
  3. Combat Trophy Farming (2–3 hours): Find a good checkpoint with multiple enemies (Chapter 8 or 11 recommended) and methodically unlock remaining combat trophies.
  4. TacSim Mode (3–5 hours): Complete Operations and Escalations up to Tier 3.
  5. Platinum Pops!
Fastest Platinum Path: Play on the easiest difficulty. Use Chapter Select immediately after the credits roll. A dedicated player can achieve Platinum in 30 hours.
