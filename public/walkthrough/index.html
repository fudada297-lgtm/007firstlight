007 First Light — Full Mission Walkthrough

Complete step-by-step guide for all missions. The game features "wide-linear" sandbox design — many missions have multiple approach paths (stealth, social, combat). This walkthrough covers the most reliable routes while noting alternative options.

Chapter 1: Against the Odds (Iceland)

Bond serves in the Royal Navy when his helicopter is shot down by missiles. He crashes on an Icelandic coastline and must survive behind enemy lines.

Part 1: Find the Antidote

Objective: Locate the nerve agent antidote after exposure.

  1. Ashore: After washing up on the beach, walk along the shore. You'll find a fallen teammate's body.
  2. First enemies: Sprint to the cover ahead when enemies appear. Stay hidden behind the barrier.
  3. Move toward the fire: Once the patrol passes, sneak toward the flames in the distance.
  4. Crouch through grass: Stay low and move through the grass. Climb up the rocky slope ahead.
  5. Rock climbing: Scale the cliff face. Bond will slip — follow the water downstream.
  6. Infiltrate: Wait for the guard to leave, then climb through the window into Room A2.
  7. Grab a disguise: Investigate the bathroom — there's a body and a gun. Exit through the right-side door. You're now wearing a disguise and enemies treat you as friendly.
  8. Card: 7 of Acorns: Enter Room A4 across from you. On the shelf to the right of the computer console.
  9. Move the vehicle: Operate the computer terminal to move the truck blocking the road.
  10. The antidote: Enter the tent, investigate the crashed drone. After the poisoning cutscene, enter the red door. Search the green container for the antidote.

Part 2: Rescue the Hostages

Objective: Free captured researchers from the camp.

  1. Enter the camp: Jump down into the enemy camp area.
  2. Legacy: Cruciform Key: Inside Container F8, on a shelf.
  3. Postcard: Iceland: Exit F8, turn right. Container B2 — on the desk.
  4. Rescue B4 and G9: Two groups of hostages. You choose the order. B4 gives you a key for G9.
  5. Memento: Satellite Part: Inside Container G9 (story objective), directly in front of you.
  6. Escape: Loosen the antenna cable. Fight through or stealth past remaining enemies to exfiltrate.
Note: The order in which you rescue the two hostage groups is a meaningful choice. Different dialogue and minor mission variations result from your decision.

Chapter 2: In His Majesty's Secret Service

A story-focused chapter with no combat or collectibles. Bond reports back to MI6 and receives his next assignment.

Note: No collectibles in this chapter.

Chapter 3: The Needle's Eye (Malta Training)

Bond arrives at the Malta training facility. This chapter introduces core mechanics in a controlled environment.

  • Postcard: Malta: Checkpoint 01 (Basic Training) — on a fold-out table near the water bottles at the training ground.
  • Follow Greenway through basic training exercises: movement, cover, stealth, and combat fundamentals.

Chapter 4: The Heart of the Matter (Q-Lab)

Bond visits Q-Branch for the first time. Meet Q and Selina Tan. Get introduced to the gadget system.

  • Intel: Workshop Notice (Selina Tan): Checkpoint 04 (Q-Lab) — back right corner cupboard where two scientists are talking.

Chapter 5: A New Home

Bond gets his first proper field assignment. Infiltrate a compound to capture a flag — training exercise by day, real infiltration by night.

  • Intel: Family Photo (Lennox Monroe): Checkpoint 01 (Home) — living room cupboard, right corner.
  • Intel: Dress Box (Cressida Bright): Checkpoint 01 — gift box on Cressida's bed.
  • Card: 7 of Roses: Checkpoint 10 (Compound) — pier area, inside double doors on pallets in room with 3 enemies.

Chapter 6: A Night Out (London Nightclub)

Bond goes undercover at a London nightclub to make contact and investigate Joshua Pike.

  • Memento: Cocktail Napkin: Checkpoint 05 (Get Upstairs) — behind the bar counter.
  • Card: Ober of Bells: Checkpoint 05 — disco room, behind the stage. Climb up scaffolding and pipes to reach it.
  • Contact in red: Find the contact in the red dress. She'll point you toward Pike.
  • Investigate Pike: Get upstairs. Eavesdrop, pickpocket, or bluff your way to Pike's private area.

Chapter 7: A Matter of Considerable Delicacy

Return to Q-Lab for mission briefing and operations planning. Moneypenny plays a key role.

  • Legacy: Dagger Shoes: Checkpoint 03 (Field Equipment) — Q-07 Wearables display, on the table after Q's cutscene.
  • Intel: Arrowhead Report: Checkpoint 07 (Operations) — opposite direction from Moneypenny's pod, desk at end of floor.
  • Intel: 009 Profile: Checkpoint 08 (Master Manipulator) — Moneypenny's office desk.

Chapter 8: All the Time in the World (Slovakia Hotel)

One of the game's standout missions. Bond poses as a chauffeur at a chess tournament in a Slovakian alpine hotel to track down the rogue agent 009.

Key Events

  1. Arrival: Park the car and enter the hotel. Your cover is a chauffeur.
  2. Card: Ace of Acorns: Checkpoint 02 (In Position) — after parking, go upstairs then down the left stairs. On the railing.
  3. Memento: Pillow Mint: Checkpoint 03 — pickpocket a key from a staffer near the stairs. Use the "Staff Only" room — on a wooden cabinet.
  4. Legacy: Chess Biography: Checkpoint 03 — down left stairs, right at bottom. End of hallway seating area — on a small table.
  5. Card: Ober of Shields: Checkpoint 03 — dining room with 7 round tables. Front left corner table.
  6. Manager's Office: Find the password 1805 for the laundry room on the manager's desk. The office safe code is 1952 (vintage year of the wine bottle).
  7. Dining Room Puzzle: Fireplace puzzle — solution is Lion → Snake → Goat (order doesn't matter).
  8. Room 206: Pickpocket Room 205 keycard from a maid. Postcard: Slovakia on the nightstand in Room 205.
  9. Car chase: After the hotel sequence, a lengthy cinematic car chase through mountain roads. Then to the airport runway for a shootout.
  10. Cargo plane finale: Hack the plane's systems with Q-Watch, causing turbulence. Fight to the end, then parachute from the plane mid-flight.
All passwords for this chapter: Laundry door 1805 · Manager safe 1952 · Fireplace Lion → Snake → Goat

Chapter 9: Out of the Ashes

Return to Q-Lab. Critical: equip the Laser Strap at the gadget table — you'll need it for Chapters 10-12.

  • Intel: Facial Composite (Niko & Tero Murto): Checkpoint 05 (Talk to Moneypenny) — behind the elevator, back left cabinet.
Don't forget! Equip the Laser Strap at Q-Lab during this chapter. Without it, many collectibles in Chapters 10-12 are unreachable.

Chapter 10: The Past Never Dies (Aleph Black Market)

Bond infiltrates a black market auction aboard a shipwreck in Mauritania. Track down 009 through the criminal underworld.

  • Card: 6 of Bells: Checkpoint 02 (Arriving at Aleph) — shipwreck market, left path, inside bathroom.
  • Card: 9 of Acorns: Checkpoint 03 (Black Market) — inside a fish tank where guards talk. Destroy with Laser Strap.
  • Card: Ober of Acorns: Checkpoint 03 — back of truck near left market area. Laser-open cabinet on wall.
  • Intel: Delivery Box (Bawma): Checkpoint 03 — market stall directly left of shooting range, back left corner.
  • Card: Ace of Roses: Checkpoint 03 — Fight Club upper floor. Exit left of bar to roof — small corner room on a box.
  • Legacy: ATAC Device: Checkpoint 03 — below signal tower (crypto wallet objective). Drop down ledges to metal beam — on crate.
  • Memento: Pirate Grillz: Checkpoint 03 — merchant in white shirt under red umbrella (right of pawn shop). Buy after getting $100k.
  • Memento: Crocodile Tooth: Checkpoint 09 (Leaving Aleph) — on crate behind the Land Rover.
  • Card: 6 of Shields: Checkpoint 10 (Find Way Inside Shipwreck) — after zipline, on barrel before blue pipe.

Chapter 11: Uninvited (London Gala)

Bond infiltrates a lavish gala at the Webb Institute museum. One of the game's densest and most Hitman-like levels with 30+ challenges.

  • Intel: Bond Family Photo: Checkpoint 02 (Back Home) — nightstand to the right of Bond's bed.
  • Card: 8 of Acorns: Checkpoint 04 (Gala Entrance) — first hall, back right corner, stone bench right of reception.
  • Intel: Webb Industries Press Pack: Checkpoint 05 (Get Upstairs) — press room, left guarded section. Distract guard with fusebox, bluff woman.
  • Memento: Anniversary Pin: Checkpoint 05 — coat check room (left of press room entrance), squeeze through gap. On shelf.
  • Legacy: Concert Poster: Checkpoint 05 — café at back left corner of Gala, back right corner on table.
  • Maintenance room code: 3256 — found on a laptop in the courtyard library.
  • Boss fight — Tero Murto: Basement Archives. Use environmental hazards (chandeliers, electric floors, exhaust pipes). After the fight, get the keycard from the box. Memento: Tero Murto Locket in Restoration Room 3.

Chapter 12: Knightfall (City of London)

Bond assaults an executive building in the heart of London's financial district. Multiple floors of combat and stealth.

  • Postcard: City of London: Checkpoint 02 (Executive 1st Floor) — after statue boost, jump to balcony, shimmy ledge, climb window. Counter on the left.
  • Intel: Wooden Spoon (Roger Finch): Checkpoint 02 — downstairs bathroom. Kick stall for office key, enter "R. Finch" office.
  • Card: Banner of Roses: Checkpoint 02 — hallway after Greenway boosts you up. "Maintenance" room, laser-open ceiling vent.
  • Attic Safe Code: 1493 — examine the diploma (1), painting (4), video game (9), and scale model (3).

Chapter 13: Going Old School

A shorter story segment bridging chapters. No collectibles — enjoy the narrative.

Chapter 14: Time to Die (Vietnam Resort)

Bond travels to a luxury resort in Vietnam. A mix of beach paradise and deadly confrontation.

  • Memento: Diving Watch: Nightstand by Bond's bed after putting on the bowtie.
  • Card: 9 of Roses: Yoga storage room in Tranquility Cave — left counter.
  • All Key Locations: Ocean View suite, minibar area, yoga storage — explore thoroughly.

Chapter 15: Man of the Hour

Return to Q-Lab for critical developments.

  • Q-Lab Q-08 Armory code: 1324 (shooting range top score).
  • Q-Lab Q-03 Pressure Test code: 4397 (car license plate number).
  • Memento: Niko Murto Locket: Opening cutscene — on desk to the left.

Chapter 16: Wave of the Future

A high-tech facility featuring a cable car sequence. Confront the mastermind behind the conspiracy.

  • Archive Room code: 1864 — from a safe downstairs, opened with a key from the Legal Department.
  • Memento: Snow Globe: Reception desk, right side.
  • Memento: Robot Keychain: Conference room podium after climbing down the power core.

Chapter 17: For England (Finale)

The climactic finale. Defend Q-Lab and confront the final threat.

  • Memento: Dashboard Toy: Q-Lab wardrobe room — model car on the table across from the changing rooms.
  • Final boss encounter. Multiple phases. Use everything you've learned.
After completing the story: Chapter Select unlocks from the main menu. Use this to replay any mission for missed collectibles, challenges, or trophies. Nothing is permanently missable.
